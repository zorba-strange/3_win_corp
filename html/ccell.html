About CCELL™

The CCell technology presents an innovated coil-embedded ceramic heating element, providing the most consistent temperature for the most reliable vaping experience. The porous ceramic cylinder coupled with the four large carburetor holes ensure optimal oil flow through the atomizer, allowing for huge pulls while simultaneously keeping oil waste to the bare minimum. The CCells inhale activation technology, paired with the high voltage battery provides a vape pen ready to hit as soon as you pick it up. Designed for big hits and full flavor profile.
